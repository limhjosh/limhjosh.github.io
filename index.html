About Me

I graduated from UCSD in 2012 and felt a calling to serve my country. I scored 98/99 on the ASVAB, allowing me to enlist in military intelligence. From there, I achieved an Associate's Degree in Intelligence Operations and obtained fluency in a foreign language to better support my team. Eventually, I was selected to work alongside strategic leaders in U.S. Pacific Command, CYBERCOM, NSA, DIA, CIA, and FBI. As my contract with the military was ending, I realized that I especially enjoyed working with computers and technology. I began to teach myself algorithms and programming syntax. Next, I took formal classes that tought me Web-Development, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX, Ruby on Rails, SQL, and MVC architecture. Subsequently, I began building and collaborating on several projects; I gained massive experience in Engineering Empathy, Agile workflow, Web APIs, React-Native, Cloud Deployment, and Full-Stack development. Currently, I am available to put my skills to work for you!
