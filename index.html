About Josh Lim

After graduating from UCSD in 2012, I felt a calling to serve my country. A 98/99 score on the ASVAB allowed me to enlist in military intelligence. From there, I achieved an Associate's Degree in Army Intelligence Operations and obtained fluency in an another foreign language to better support my team. Eventually, I was selected to work alongside strategic leaders who collaborate with the FBI, CIA, DIA, and NSA. As my contract with the military was coming to an end, I realized that I especially enjoyed working with computers and technology. First, I began to teach myself algorithms and programming syntax. Next, I took formal classes that tought me Web-Development, Javascript, jQuery, AJAX, SQL, and MVC architecture. Subsequently, I began building and collaborating on several projects; I gained massive experience in Engineering Empathy, Agile workflow, Web APIs, React-Native, Cloud Deployment, and Full-Stack development. Currently, I am available to put my skills to work for you!
